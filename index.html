Maximus

Maximus is the distinguished mathematics club and society at the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli, serving as a hub for fostering a deep appreciation and understanding of mathematics among its members. Through an array of activities such as seminars, workshops, problem-solving sessions, and competitions, Maximus is dedicated to nurturing mathematical proficiency and honing critical thinking skills among its participants.

Functioning as a catalyst for intellectual exchange and exploration, Maximus continually endeavors to broaden its horizons. The club's aspirations extend towards forging collaborations with other esteemed academic institutions, hosting regional and national-level events, and intensifying its focus on community outreach initiatives. These endeavors aim to inspire and educate aspiring mathematicians while further solidifying Maximus's position as a prominent mathematical society.

With an unwavering dedication to cultivating mathematical excellence, Maximus envisions a vibrant mathematical community that celebrates innovation, scholarly achievements, and inclusivity. As it strides forward, the society remains committed to fostering an environment where mathematical passion thrives, and where individuals can contribute actively to the ever-evolving world of mathematics.

About Us

Maximus is a close-knit community of philomaths, allured by the paradoxes of mathematics. Since its founding in 2015, Maximus has taken a profusion of initiatives to bestir the inquisitiveness for maths among the students of our college. One such initiative is conducting 'Engineering Mathematics' and 'Programming in C' classes for the first-year students of our college. With the 'Maths Week' being the highlight of our mettle, we conduct events like workshops, hackathons, and several other contests throughout the year to explore the bounds of mathematics
